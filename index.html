Intelligent Autism Communication Aid via Brain-Computer Interface (BCI)

About Us

Our Mission

To bridge the communication gap between non-verbal autistic children and their caregivers by providing a passive, real-time, brain-computer interface that translates thoughts into words during moments of greatest need.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their ability to speak, has a voice that is heard, understood, and valued—empowering families, educators, and specialists with the tools to connect, support, and care without guesswork.

Key Features

Brainwave Mapping

Translates brain signals into structured, meaningful phrases, bridging the gap between nonverbal expression and clear communication.

HAWER

A speech-guided interface for conversational support and echolalia management.

Arabic PECS Library

A customizable, digitized library of PECS cards specifically tailored for Arabic users.

Emotiv Integration

Seamlessly integrated with the Emotiv Insight headset to capture and classify commands.
